After careful review of the prior authorization request submitted on April 10, 2025, 
MedAdvantage Premier Plan has determined that the requested service does not meet the 
criteria for medical necessity under your member's benefit plan.

REASON FOR DENIAL:

1. MEDICAL NECESSITY NOT ESTABLISHED
   The clinical documentation submitted does not sufficiently establish that 
   Pembrolizumab is medically necessary for this patient at this time. The submitted 
   notes do not include objective measurable evidence that the requested treatment is 
   the most clinically appropriate option.

2. STEP THERAPY REQUIREMENTS NOT MET
   Per MedAdvantage Premier Plan formulary policy (Policy MP-ONC-2024-07), 
   immunotherapy agents including Pembrolizumab require documented failure of at least 
   one standard platinum-based chemotherapy regimen. The submitted documentation does 
   not include adequate evidence of prior chemotherapy trial and documented failure.

3. MISSING DOCUMENTATION
   The following documentation was not included or was insufficient:
   - PD-L1 expression test results with TPS score
   - Documented reason for discontinuation of prior chemotherapy
   - Treating physician statement of medical necessity
   - ECOG performance status assessment

APPEAL RIGHTS:

You have the right to appeal this decision. Your appeal must be submitted within 
30 days of the date of this letter (by May 15, 2025). 

To submit an appeal, send the following to appeals@medadvantagepremier.com:
- Written appeal letter explaining why the denial should be reversed
- All missing documentation listed above
- Any additional clinical information supporting medical necessity

An expedited appeal may be requested if a standard appeal timeframe would seriously 
jeopardize the patient's life, health, or ability to regain maximum function.
